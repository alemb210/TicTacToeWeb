Tic-Tac-Toe

Instructions for Tic-Tac-Toe:
1. Pick a size for the board.
This will determine the length/width of the board (ex. size 5 is a 5x5 board)
2. Click in any box to make your move. You will be Player O.
3. Wait for the computer, Player X, to move.
4. The first player to connect a whole line wins!
5. Restart Page to play again :)
